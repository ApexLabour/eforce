About E-Force Security Services

E-Force Security Services is a proudly Level 1 B-BBEE contributor, founded by Devan Naidoo in November 2013 after seeing that our local security industry lacks passion, training and professionalism.

We specialise in:

  • 24 hour on-site guarding services.
  • Armed escort and tuck-in services.
  • VIP protection and body guarding services.
  • 24 hour surveillance services.
  • Basic firefighting and evacuation drills.

It was decided to start this company with a vision to provide security staffing of a superior quality that will exceed our customer’s expectations and that of our competitors hence the name Elite Force where we trade as E-Force Security (Pty) Ltd.

24 Hour On-Site Security Guard Service

Our 24 hour security guards can be armed or unarmed and are supported 24/7 by our 24 hour armed response and a state-of-the-art control room.

Armed Escort Services

Plain clothes or uniformed armed escort services are provided by rigorously trained professionals after extensive background checks.

VIP Protection Services

Executive VIP protection services available for executives, travellers, entertainers and other VIPs requiring personal protection.

24 Hour Surveillance Services

By providing access to your site's CCTV network, our state-of-the-art control can maintain surveillance on your site 24/7.

24 Hour On-Site Guarding Services

At E-Force Security Services we supply fully trained, qualified, PSIRA accredited security guards.

Our guards will assist with hourly or bi-hourly site patrols, site access control for vehicles and walk-ins.

Armed Escort Services

E-Force Security provides armed escort services to facilitate transportation of high value goods or personnel. We provide trained, highly skilled guards to meet the stringent requirements expected.

VIP Protection Services

Also known as close protection, encompasses a range of security measures designed to ensure the safety and privacy of prominent individuals, including personal security details and secure transportation.

Personal Body Guards

Similar to VIP protection services, personal bodyguards protect and guard individuals. Offering tailored security solutions, including close protection, risk assessment, and threat mitigation.

24 Hour Surveillance

E-Force surveillance services include monitoring activities and behaviors to gather information, with access to CCTV cameras and a state-of-the-art control room provide 24/7 peace of mind.

Basic Firefighting and Evacuation

We provide basic firefighting services in line with emergency response protocols and building/site evacuation drills training to ensure your team have a plan ready if the need should arise.
